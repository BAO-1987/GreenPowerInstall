Greener Future at Green Galaxy Shopping Center

Uncover how the Greengalaxy Shopping Center embraced solar energy to reduce operating costs and set an example for sustainable retail practices.

The Green Galaxy Shopping Center project represents a landmark achievement in sustainable commercial development. Through meticulous planning and execution, we installed a large-scale solar power station atop the shopping center's rooftop.

A significant reduction in the shopping center's carbon footprint, along with substantial energy savings and increased sustainability. This project showcases our expertise in delivering scalable solar solutions for commercial clients, driving positive environmental impact and financial savings.
